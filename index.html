I'm a fifth-year Ph.D. student at the College of Information Sciences and Technology, Pennsylvania State University, working with Prof. C. Lee Giles. Before joining Penn State, I received my Bachelor degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University in 2013.

My research interests lie in natural language processing and machine learning. During my Ph.D. study, I develop NLP/ML methods for building educational applications. Specifically, I work on computer-facilitated book creation, concept prerequisite learning, and fill-in-the-blank question generation.
