[Curriculum Vitae]


I'm a research scientist at Meta (previously known as Facebook) in the ads core ML team working on ads ranking personalization. I conduct research on user modeling to improve ads ranking model performance. Before joining Meta, I obtained my Ph.D. from the College of Information Sciences and Technology, Pennsylvania State University, under the supervision of Prof. C. Lee Giles. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University in 2013.

I'm passionate about applied machine learning. During my Ph.D. study, I developed machine learning methods for building educational applications. Specifically, I worked on concept prerequisite learning, fill-in-the-blank question generation, and computer-facilitated book creation. Please check out these projects to learn more.
